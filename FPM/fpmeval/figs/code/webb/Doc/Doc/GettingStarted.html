Getting Started with the Magnum Opus Command Line System

Magnum Opus delivers the power of the most recent advances in association discovery technology.  With unrivalled flexibility and ease-of-use, it finds new and unanticipated associations from data. Use Magnum Opus to unlock the wealth of information implicit within your data assets.

Magnum Opus employs unique k-optimal (also known as top-k) association discovery techniques.  Tell Magnum Opus what makes a association interesting to you and how many you would like.   Magnum Opus delivers the associations that are most interesting according to the measure that you select.  Magnum Opus does all the hard work but lets you make all the decisions.

Other systems either employ undisclosed proprietary heuristics that select a few associations for you, or indiscriminately present countless associations that bury the gems amongst the dross.  In contrast, Magnum Opus will always deliver exactly the associations that maximize the measure that you select, within whatever other constraints you choose.  These further constraints include unrivalled facilities for detecting and discarding spurious 'discoveries'.

Magnum Opus is the only system that supports statistically sound association discovery.

Some important features:

  • Magnum Opus is easy to use and does not presume advanced knowledge of statistics or machine learning.
  • Magnum Opus associations are expressed in forms that are simple to understand.
  • Magnum Opus includes a unique facility to automatically filter out associations that are unlikely to be of interest, and includes the unique ability to perform statistically sound exploratory association discovery.
  • Unlike other association discovery systems, Magnum Opus does not rely on sparse data for efficient processing.
  • Magnum Opus finds associations from both transaction data and attribute-value data.
  • Magnum Opus has been designed to analyze substantial databases containing millions of records.
  • Magnum Opus is fast. It has linear compute time. So long as your data fit in your computer's physical memory, if the amount of data is doubled then the compute time will approximately double.
  • Magnum Opus is flexible.  The different search options support a wide range of differing analytic objectives.
