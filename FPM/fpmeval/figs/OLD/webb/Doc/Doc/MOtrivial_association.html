Redundant rule

A rule x is redundant if there exists another rule y that is identical except that the LHS of y contains a subset of the LHS of x and both rules cover exactly the same examples in the data file. The extra attribute value(s) in the LHS of the redundant rule provide no additional lift or leverage. Redundant rules are discarded by Magnum Opus under all filter modes other than none.

In previous versions of Magnum Opus redundant rules were called trivial rules.
